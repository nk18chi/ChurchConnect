## Getting Started

### Logging In

1. Visit https://portal.churchconnect.jp
2. Enter your email address and password
3. Click "Sign In"

**Screenshot location: Login page with email/password fields**

1. Click "Forgot Password" on the login page

4. Follow the link and create a new password

## Profile Setup

Complete all 10 sections for maximum visibility in search results. Each section helps visitors understand your church better.

### Section 1: Basic Information

**How to edit:**
1. Click "Profile" in the sidebar
2. Scroll to "Basic Information"
3. Fill in or update:
   - **Church Name**: Your official church name
   - **Short Description**: 1-2 sentence summary (appears in search results)
   - **Address**: Full street address including city and prefecture
   - **Phone Number**: Best contact number
   - **Email**: General inquiry email
   - **Website**: Your church website URL (if you have one)
4. Click "Save Changes"

**Screenshot location: Basic Information form fields**

**Tips:**
- Use your official registered church name
- Keep the short description welcoming and clear
- Double-check your address for accuracy (it's used for the map)
- Use an email address that's checked regularly

### Section 2: Service Times

**How to add service times:**
1. Navigate to "Profile" ‚Üí "Service Times"
2. Click "Add Service Time"
3. Fill in:
   - **Day of Week**: Select from dropdown
   - **Time**: Use 24-hour format or select from picker
   - **Service Type**: Sunday Worship, Prayer Meeting, Bible Study, etc.
   - **Language**: Primary language of this service
   - **Notes**: Optional (e.g., "Children's program available")
4. Click "Add"

**Screenshot location: Add Service Time dialog**

**Managing multiple services:**
- Add each service separately (e.g., 9:00 AM English, 11:00 AM Japanese)
- Services display in chronological order
- Click "Edit" or "Delete" to modify existing services

**Tips:**
- Include all regular services (weekly, monthly)
- Specify language for multilingual churches
- Add notes for special features (childcare, translation, etc.)

### Section 3: Who We Are

**How to edit:**
1. Go to "Profile" ‚Üí "Who We Are"
2. Use the rich text editor to describe:
   - Your church's mission and values
   - What makes your community special
   - What newcomers can expect
   - Your church culture and atmosphere
3. Click "Save"

**Screenshot location: Who We Are editor with formatting toolbar**

**Writing tips:**
- Be warm and welcoming
- Use specific examples and stories
- Keep it concise (2-3 paragraphs ideal)
- Highlight what makes you unique
- Answer: "Why would someone want to visit our church?"

**Example:**
```
We are a diverse, international community of believers seeking to follow
Jesus together in Tokyo. With services in both English and Japanese, we
welcome people from all backgrounds and walks of life. Our heart is to
create a space where everyone can encounter God's love, grow in faith,
and find genuine community.
```

**Tips:**
- Make it inspiring and forward-looking
- Be specific about your community impact goals
- Connect vision to your daily ministry
- Keep it authentic to your context

### Section 5: Statement of Faith

**How to edit:**
1. Go to "Profile" ‚Üí "Statement of Faith"
2. Outline your core theological beliefs
3. Include:
   - View of Scripture

### Section 6: Our Story

1. Navigate to "Profile" ‚Üí "Our Story"

## Content Management

### Managing Photos

Photos bring your profile to life! Visitors are more likely to engage with profiles that have lots of quality photos.

**How to upload photos:**

1. Click "Photos" in the sidebar
2. Click "Upload Photo" button
3. Select an image from your computer
4. Choose a category:
   - **Worship Service**: Sunday gatherings, worship teams
   - **Fellowship**: Community meals, coffee time, social events
   - **Ministry**: Small groups, classes, outreach activities
   - **Building/Facility**: Exterior, sanctuary, children's rooms
   - **Events**: Special occasions, conferences, celebrations
   - **Other**: Anything else
5. Add a caption (recommended)
6. Click "Upload"

**Screenshot location: Photo upload dialog with category selection**

**Photo requirements:**
- Maximum file size: 5MB
- Accepted formats: JPG, PNG, WebP
- Recommended: High resolution (at least 1200px wide)
- Landscape orientation works best

**Photo tips:**
- Show your community, not just the building
- Include diverse representation
- Capture authentic moments
- Use good lighting
- Take photos during services (with permission)
- Update seasonally

**Managing existing photos:**
- **Edit caption**: Hover over photo ‚Üí Click "Edit" ‚Üí Update caption ‚Üí Save
- **Delete photo**: Hover over photo ‚Üí Click "Delete" ‚Üí Confirm
- **Reorder**: Drag photos to change order (first photo shows as thumbnail)

**Screenshot location: Photo gallery with hover actions visible**

**Privacy considerations:**
- Get permission before posting photos with children
- Consider who's recognizable in your photos
- Have a photo release policy for your church

### Managing Staff

Introduce your leadership team to help visitors connect with your church.

**How to add staff members:**

1. Click "Staff" in the sidebar
2. Click "Add Staff Member"
3. Fill in details:
   - **Name**: Full name
   - **Title**: E.g., "Senior Pastor", "Worship Leader"
   - **Role Category**: Leadership, Ministry, Administration
   - **Bio**: Their story, background, and ministry focus
   - **Email**: Contact email (optional)
   - **Photo**: Upload a professional headshot
4. Click "Save"

**Screenshot location: Add Staff Member form**

**Writing staff bios:**
- Keep it personal and warm (3-5 sentences)
- Include background and how they came to your church
- Mention their ministry passions
- Add personal touches (family, hobbies)

**Example bio:**
```
Pastor John has been serving at Tokyo Community Church since 2015.
Originally from Canada, he came to Japan in 2008 and fell in love
with both the country and his wife, Yuki. John is passionate about
helping people discover Jesus and growing authentic community. When
he's not at church, you'll find him exploring Tokyo's coffee shops
or attempting to improve his Japanese.
```

**Managing staff:**
- **Edit**: Click "Edit" next to any staff member
- **Delete**: Click "Delete" (use for staff who leave)
- **Reorder**: Drag to change display order (senior leadership first)

**Screenshot location: Staff list with drag handles and action buttons**

**Tips:**
- Include all key leaders
- Update when staff changes occur
- Use high-quality, professional photos
- Keep bios current

### Managing Events

Keep your community informed about upcoming events and activities.

**How to create an event:**

1. Click "Events" in the sidebar
2. Click "Create Event"
3. Fill in event details:
   - **Event Name**: Clear, descriptive title
   - **Description**: What, why, and what to expect
   - **Start Date & Time**: When it begins
   - **End Date & Time**: When it ends (optional for single-day events)
   - **Location**:
     - "At our church" (uses your church address)
     - "Online" (for virtual events)
     - Custom address (for off-site events)
   - **Registration Required**: Yes/No
   - **Registration Link**: External link (if using online registration)
   - **Contact Email**: Who to contact with questions
   - **Event Image**: Upload an eye-catching image
4. Click "Publish"

**Screenshot location: Create Event form**

**Event status:**
- **Draft**: Not visible to public (save and finish later)
- **Published**: Visible on your public profile
- **Past**: Automatically moved to past events after end date

**Managing events:**
- **Edit**: Click "Edit" on any event
- **Duplicate**: Use for recurring events (saves time)
- **Delete**: Remove events permanently
- **Unpublish**: Hide without deleting (makes it draft again)

**Screenshot location: Events list showing draft/published/past tabs**

**Event tips:**
- Create events 2-4 weeks in advance
- Include all important details (parking, what to bring, cost)
- Update if details change
- Delete or archive past events regularly
- Use high-quality event images
- Add multiple events for recurring activities

**Event image specs:**
- Recommended size: 1200x630px
- Format: JPG or PNG
- Include event title in image if possible

### Managing Sermons

Share your sermons to extend your ministry reach and help visitors get to know your teaching.

**How to add a sermon:**

1. Click "Sermons" in the sidebar
2. Click "Add Sermon"
3. Fill in details:
   - **Title**: Sermon title

   - **Description**: Brief summary (2-3 sentences)
   - **YouTube URL**: Link to video (if available)
   - **Audio URL**: Link to podcast/audio file (if available)
   - **Transcript**: Full text (optional but helps accessibility)
4. Click "Publish"

**Screenshot location: Add Sermon form**

**Sermon series:**
- Group related sermons together
- Click "Create Series" to start a new series
- Assign sermons to series when adding/editing

**Managing sermons:**
- **Edit**: Update sermon details
- **Delete**: Remove permanently
- **Filter**: View by series, preacher, or date
- **Sort**: Most recent first (default)

**Screenshot location: Sermons list with series grouping**

**Sermon tips:**
- Add sermons weekly to keep content fresh
- Include scripture references for searchability
- Link videos when available (YouTube embeds automatically)
- Consider transcripts for accessibility
- Use consistent naming for series
- Tag by book of the Bible

**Video hosting:**
- YouTube is recommended (free, reliable)
- Upload to your church YouTube channel

## Managing Reviews

Reviews help build trust and provide authentic feedback from your community.

### Viewing Reviews

1. Click "Reviews" in the sidebar
2. View reviews organized by status:
   - **Pending**: Awaiting platform admin approval
   - **Approved**: Live on your public profile
   - **Rejected**: Not approved by platform admin

**Screenshot location: Reviews dashboard with tabs**

**Review details show:**
- Reviewer name
- Star rating (1-5 stars)
- Review text
- Visit date
- Date submitted
- Current status

### Responding to Reviews

**How to respond:**

1. Find the review (must be "Approved")
2. Click "Respond"
3. Write your response
4. Click "Submit Response"

**Screenshot location: Review response dialog**

**Response best practices:**

**For positive reviews:**
```
Thank you for your kind words! We're so glad you felt welcomed and
enjoyed the service. We hope to see you again soon!
```

**For constructive reviews:**
```
Thank you for sharing your experience and this feedback. We take
all comments seriously and will work to improve our welcome process.
Please feel free to reach out to me directly at pastor@church.com
if you'd like to discuss further.
```

**Response tips:**
- Respond within 48 hours
- Be gracious and humble
- Address specific points raised
- Avoid being defensive
- Take conversations offline when appropriate
- Thank reviewers for taking the time

**What NOT to do:**
- Don't argue or debate in responses
- Don't post personal information
- Don't question reviewer's motives
- Don't ask people to remove reviews
- Don't write overly long responses (keep to 2-3 sentences)

### Flagging Inappropriate Reviews

If a review violates guidelines:

**How to flag:**
1. Click "Flag Review" button
2. Select reason:
   - Spam or fake review
   - Offensive language
   - Personal attack
   - Irrelevant content
   - Violates terms of service
3. Add explanation (optional but helpful)
4. Submit flag

**Screenshot location: Flag review dialog with reason options**

**What happens next:**
- Platform admin reviews within 24-48 hours
- You receive notification of decision
- If removed, it disappears from your profile
- If kept, you can appeal

**Flagging guidelines:**
- Only flag genuinely inappropriate content
- Don't flag negative but honest reviews
- Provide context in your explanation
- Be patient with review process

## Verification Process

Verified churches get priority in search results and build more trust with visitors.

### Benefits of Verification

- ‚úì **Verified badge** on your profile

- **Increased trust** from visitors

### Requesting Verification

**How to apply:**

1. Click "Verification" in the sidebar
2. Complete verification checklist:
   - [ ] Profile is 100% complete
   - [ ] At least 5 photos uploaded
   - [ ] Church contact info verified
   - [ ] Operating for at least 6 months
3. Upload proof of church ownership:
   - **Official church document** (articles of incorporation)
   - **Letter on church letterhead** (signed by senior leader)
   - **Recent utility bill** showing church address

**Document requirements:**
- Must clearly show church name
- Must show church address
- Must be dated within last 12 months
- Must be legible (good quality scan/photo)
- Accepted formats: PDF, JPG, PNG

### Verification Review Process

**Timeline:**
1. **Submitted**: You receive confirmation email
2. **Under Review**: Platform admin reviews (24-48 hours)
3. **Approved or Rejected**: You receive email notification

**If approved:**
- ‚úì badge appears immediately on your profile
- You're added to verified directory
- Search ranking improves

**If rejected:**
- Email explains reason
- You can resubmit with better documentation
- No penalty for reapplying

**Common rejection reasons:**
- Incomplete profile
- Insufficient proof of ownership
- Documents unclear or outdated
- Church operating less than 6 months
- Contact information doesn't match documents

### Maintaining Verification

**Requirements to stay verified:**
- Keep profile information accurate
- Respond to platform admin inquiries
- Maintain good standing with review policies
- Update contact info if church moves

**Verification can be revoked if:**
- Profile information becomes seriously outdated
- Church closes or merges
- Serious violations of platform policies

## FAQ

### Account & Login

**Q: How do I change my password?**

A: Click your profile icon (top right) ‚Üí Settings ‚Üí Change Password ‚Üí Enter current and new password ‚Üí Save

**Q: How do I change my email address?**

A: Contact platform support at admin@churchconnect.jp (email changes require verification)

**Q: Can I have multiple admin accounts?**

A: Not currently. We're working on adding multiple admin support. For now, share login credentials securely within your team.

**Q: What if I forget my password?**

A: Click "Forgot Password" on login page ‚Üí Enter email ‚Üí Check email for reset link ‚Üí Create new password

### Profile Management

**Q: How long does it take for profile changes to appear?**

A: Changes appear immediately after clicking "Save Changes". Refresh your public profile to see updates.

**Q: Can I unpublish my church temporarily?**

A: Contact admin@churchconnect.jp to request temporary unpublishing (for renovations, pastoral transitions, etc.)

**Q: How do I change my church name?**

A: Edit under Profile ‚Üí Basic Information. Major name changes may require re-verification.

**Q: What if my church moves to a new location?**

A: Update address under Profile ‚Üí Basic Information. Update verification documents if verified.

**Q: How do I delete my church profile permanently?**

A: Contact admin@churchconnect.jp with your request. Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

### Photos & Media

**Q: Why won't my photo upload?**

A: Check that:
- File is under 5MB
- Format is JPG, PNG, or WebP
- You have stable internet connection
- Try a different browser if issues persist

**Q: Can I upload videos directly?**

A: No, but you can link to YouTube videos in sermons and events. We recommend hosting videos on YouTube.

**Q: How many photos can I upload?**

A: Unlimited! We encourage lots of photos to showcase your community.

**Q: Can I bulk upload photos?**

A: Not yet, but this feature is planned. For now, upload one at a time.

### Events & Content

**Q: How do I create recurring events?**

A: Use the "Duplicate" feature on an existing event, then update the date. We're working on automatic recurring event support.

**Q: Can visitors register for events on ChurchConnect?**

A: Not directly. Add an external registration link (Google Forms, Eventbrite, etc.) to your event.

**Q: How far in advance should I post events?**

A: Post 2-4 weeks in advance for best visibility. Events show in search results and on your profile.

**Q: Should I delete past events?**

A: Past events move automatically to "Past Events" section. You can delete them or keep them for historical record.

### Reviews

**Q: Can I delete a negative review?**

A: No, but you can flag inappropriate reviews for admin review. Honest negative reviews remain to maintain authenticity.

**Q: Why is a review "Pending"?**

A: All reviews go through platform admin moderation before appearing publicly. This prevents spam and maintains quality.

**Q: Can I edit my response to a review?**

A: Yes, click "Edit Response" on any review you've responded to.

**Q: What if someone posts false information in a review?**

A: Respond professionally to correct the information, and flag the review if it violates guidelines.

### Verification

**Q: How long does verification take?**

A: Usually 24-48 hours. Complex cases may take up to 5 business days.

**Q: Do I need to be verified?**

A: No, but verified churches rank higher in search and build more trust.

**Q: What if I don't have official church documents?**

A: Contact admin@churchconnect.jp to discuss alternative verification methods.

**Q: Does verification cost money?**

A: No, verification is completely free.

### Technical Issues

**Q: The site is slow or not loading**

A:
1. Check your internet connection
2. Try a different browser
3. Clear browser cache and cookies
4. Contact support if issues persist

**Q: I'm getting an error when saving**

A:
1. Check all required fields are filled
2. Ensure file uploads are under size limits
3. Try refreshing the page
4. Contact support with error message screenshot

**Q: Changes aren't saving**

A:
1. Make sure you clicked "Save Changes"
2. Check for error messages
3. Verify all required fields are completed
4. Try a different browser

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### Can't Login

**Problem:** Email/password not working

**Solutions:**
1. Check caps lock is off
2. Verify email address is correct
3. Use "Forgot Password" to reset
4. Clear browser cookies
5. Try incognito/private browsing mode
6. Contact admin if still blocked

#### Photo Upload Fails

**Problem:** Photo won't upload or shows error

**Solutions:**
1. **Check file size**: Must be under 5MB
   - Reduce size using photo editor
   - Use compression tool (TinyPNG, etc.)
2. **Check file format**: Must be JPG, PNG, or WebP
   - Convert using online converter
3. **Check internet**: Ensure stable connection
4. **Try different photo**: May be file corruption
5. **Try different browser**: Chrome works best

#### Changes Not Saving

**Problem:** Edits don't appear after clicking save

**Solutions:**
1. **Check for errors**: Red error messages on form
2. **Fill required fields**: Look for asterisks (*)
3. **Check internet**: Must have stable connection
4. **Refresh page**: Hard refresh (Ctrl+Shift+R or Cmd+Shift+R)
5. **Try again**: May be temporary server issue

#### Profile Not Appearing in Search

**Problem:** Can't find church in directory search

**Solutions:**
1. **Check published status**: Profile must be published
2. **Complete profile**: Incomplete profiles rank lower
3. **Wait 24 hours**: New profiles take time to index
4. **Check search terms**: Try different keywords
5. **Get verified**: Verified churches rank higher

#### Email Not Received

**Problem:** Not getting notification emails

**Solutions:**
1. **Check spam folder**: May be filtered as spam
2. **Verify email**: Check Settings for correct email
3. **Add to contacts**: Add noreply@churchconnect.jp
4. **Wait longer**: Emails can take 5-10 minutes
5. **Contact support**: If missing important emails
