## Getting Started

### What is ChurchConnect Japan?

ChurchConnect Japan is a free directory that helps you:
- **Discover churches** across Japan
- **Read authentic reviews** from community members
- **Learn about churches** before visiting
- **Connect with churches** directly
- **Support our ministry** through donations

**No account required to browse!** You only need an account to write reviews.

### Who is this for?

**For visitors:**
- New to Japan and looking for a church
- Moving to a new city
- Exploring different denominations
- Seeking a specific worship style or language

**For church seekers:**
- Want to read reviews before visiting
- Looking for specific programs (youth, children, international)
- Seeking churches that speak your language
- Want to understand church beliefs before visiting

### How to Use ChurchConnect

**Browse without an account:**
1. Visit https://churchconnect.jp
2. Search for churches
3. View church profiles
4. Read reviews
5. Contact churches directly

**Create an account to:**
- Write reviews
- Save favorite churches (coming soon)
- Track churches you've visited (coming soon)

## Finding Churches

### Homepage Search

**Quick search:**
1. Enter a keyword in the search box:
   - Church name: "Tokyo Baptist"
   - City: "Shibuya"
   - Denomination: "Presbyterian"
   - Language: "English"
2. Click "Search" or press Enter

**Screenshot location: Homepage search bar**

**Search tips:**
- Use English or Japanese
- Try different keywords (area, denomination, language)
- Don't worry about exact spelling
- Broader searches return more results

### Advanced Search

**Using filters:**
1. Click "Advanced Search" or scroll to filters
2. Apply filters:
   - **Location**: Prefecture, City
   - **Denomination**: Select from list (Baptist, Presbyterian, etc.)
   - **Language**: English, Japanese, Korean, Chinese, etc.
   - **Worship Style**: Contemporary, Traditional, Blended
   - **Verified Only**: Show only verified churches

3. Click "Apply Filters"

**Screenshot location: Advanced search filters panel**

**Filter combinations:**
- Location + Language (e.g., Tokyo + English)
- Denomination + Worship Style (e.g., Baptist + Contemporary)
- Any combination that matches your preferences

### Search Results

**Understanding results:**

Each result shows:
- **Church Name**: Official church name
- **Short Description**: 1-2 sentence summary
- **Location**: City, Prefecture
- **Rating**: Average star rating (if reviews exist)
- **Verified Badge**: ‚úì if church is verified
- **Service Times**: Primary service time
- **Languages**: Languages offered

**Screenshot location: Search results list**

**Result actions:**
- Click church name to view full profile
- See location on map (if map view enabled)
- Note verified badge for verified churches

**Sorting options:**
- **Relevance**: Best match for your search (default)
- **Rating**: Highest rated first
- **Recent**: Recently updated profiles
- **Distance**: Nearest first (if using location)

**No results?**
- Broaden your search (fewer filters)
- Check spelling
- Try different keywords
- Browse all churches (remove all filters)

### Map View

**Finding churches near you:**
1. Click "Map View" toggle
2. Allow location access (optional)
3. See churches plotted on map
4. Click markers to see church info
5. Click church name to view full profile

**Screenshot location: Map view with church markers**

**Map features:**
- Zoom in/out to see more/fewer churches
- Click clusters to expand
- Use location services to find nearby churches
- Switch back to list view anytime

### Browse by Category

**Browse specific categories:**
1. Click "Browse" in navigation
2. Select category:
   - **By Location**: All prefectures
   - **By Denomination**: All denominations
   - **By Language**: All languages

## Writing Reviews

Help others discover churches by sharing your experience!

### Creating an Account

**Required to write reviews:**

1. Click "Sign In" (top right)
2. Click "Create Account"
3. Fill in:
   - Name (first and last)
   - Email address
   - Password (8+ characters)
4. Agree to terms of service
5. Click "Create Account"
6. Check email for verification link
7. Click link to verify email

**Screenshot location: Sign up form**

**Account benefits:**
- Write reviews
- Edit your reviews
- Track your review history
- Receive review notifications

**Privacy:**
- Your email is never shared
- Your name appears on reviews
- You can use a nickname (update in settings)

### Writing a Review

**How to review:**

1. Log in to your account
2. Navigate to church profile
3. Scroll to Reviews section
4. Click "Write a Review"
5. Fill in review form:
   - **Star Rating**: 1-5 stars (required)
   - **Visit Date**: When did you attend? (required)
   - **Review Title**: Short summary (optional)
   - **Review Text**: Your experience (required)
   - **Would you recommend?**: Yes/No (optional)
6. Click "Submit Review"

**Screenshot location: Write review form**

### Review Guidelines

**What makes a good review:**

**Be Specific:**
```
Bad: "Good church."
Good: "Warm, welcoming community with excellent children's
program. Pastor's teaching is biblically sound and applicable.
Sunday service is in English with Japanese translation available."
```

**Be Honest:**
- Share your genuine experience
- Mention both positives and areas for improvement
- Be fair and balanced

**Be Helpful:**
- Include details others would want to know
- Mention aspects like:
  - Worship style
  - Friendliness
  - Teaching quality
  - Programs available
  - Parking/accessibility
  - Language support

**Be Respectful:**
- Use kind, constructive language
- Avoid personal attacks
- Focus on your experience
- Disagree graciously with doctrine

**Example good review:**
```
Title: Welcoming International Church

We visited Tokyo Community Church for the first time last Sunday
and felt immediately welcomed. The worship was contemporary with
a live band, and the pastor's message was biblically solid and
easy to understand. They offer English and Japanese services, and
we attended the English service which had about 100 people. The
children's program was excellent - our kids loved it! After the
service, several people introduced themselves and invited us for
coffee. We'll definitely be back.

Helpful info: Parking is limited, but there's a coin parking
nearby. They also livestream services if you can't attend in person.
```

**Screenshot location: Example of good review**

**Avoid these in reviews:**

‚úÖ **Better:**

‚ùå **Hate speech or offensive language**
- Never acceptable

‚ùå **Spam or promotional content**
```

```

‚ùå **Completely irrelevant information**
- Stay focused on the church experience

‚ùå **False information**
- Only review if you actually attended
- Be truthful about your experience

### Review Moderation

**What happens after you submit:**

1. **Moderation**: Platform admin reviews (24-48 hours)
2. **Approval**: Review appears on church profile
3. **Notification**: You receive email confirmation
4. **Church Response**: Church may respond to your review

**Screenshot location: Review pending moderation message**

**Why moderation?**
- Ensures review quality
- Prevents spam and fake reviews
- Maintains platform integrity
- Protects churches from abuse

**If your review is rejected:**
- You'll receive email with reason
- Common reasons: spam, offensive language, not relevant
- You can rewrite and resubmit

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: Is ChurchConnect free?**
A: Yes! ChurchConnect is completely free for both churches and users. We're supported by donations.

**Q: Do I need an account to browse churches?**
A: No! You can search and view churches without an account. You only need an account to write reviews.

**Q: How do you verify churches?**
A: Churches submit verification requests with documentation proving ownership. Our admin team reviews and approves legitimate churches.

**Q: Can I trust the reviews?**
A: All reviews are moderated by our team before appearing. We filter spam, fake reviews, and inappropriate content to maintain authenticity.

**Q: How often is information updated?**
A: Church admins can update their profiles anytime. We encourage them to keep information current.

### Using the Site

**Q: How do I search for English-speaking churches?**
A: Use the language filter in Advanced Search and select "English".

**Q: Can I search by train station?**
A: Not directly, but you can search by city name or use the map view to find churches near a station.

**Q: Why doesn't a church have any reviews?**
A: The church may be new to the platform, or community members haven't written reviews yet. You can be the first!

**Q: Can I save favorite churches?**
A: This feature is coming soon! Currently, you can bookmark church pages in your browser.

**Q: How do I report a problem with a church profile?**
A: Email support@churchconnect.jp with details about the issue.

### Reviews

**Q: Can I review a church I only attended once?**
A: Yes! Even one-time visits are valuable reviews.

**Q: How long until my review appears?**
A: Reviews are typically approved within 24-48 hours.

**Q: Can I leave an anonymous review?**
A: No, reviews require an account and your name appears on the review. This maintains authenticity.

**Q: What if the church responds to my review?**
A: You'll receive an email notification. The response appears below your review on their profile.

**Q: Can I delete my review?**
A: Yes, from your "My Reviews" page. Deletion is permanent.

### Technical

**Q: Which browsers are supported?**
A: ChurchConnect works best on modern browsers: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge (latest versions).

**Q: Does the site work on mobile?**
A: Yes! The site is fully responsive and works great on phones and tablets.

**Q: I'm getting an error. What should I do?**
A: Try refreshing the page, clearing your browser cache, or trying a different browser. If the problem persists, email support@churchconnect.jp.

**Q: Is my information secure?**
A: Yes. We use industry-standard encryption and security practices. We never sell or share your personal information.

### For Churches

**Q: How can our church get listed?**
A: Contact admin@churchconnect.jp to request a church profile.

**Q: How much does it cost for churches?**
A: ChurchConnect is completely free for all churches!

**Q: How do we update our church information?**
A: Church admins receive login credentials to manage their profiles through the church portal.

**Q: How do we become verified?**
A: Church admins can submit a verification request through their portal with proof of church ownership.
